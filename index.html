Previously a physicist trying to ride the deep learning bandwagon, a year long research stint (with my now PhD advisor) converted me to mathematical logic. I have ever since worked on aspects of learning, reasoning and inference in probabilistic logic models. Most of my work reduces to fundamental problems in first-order logic, probability theory, and elementary combinatorics.
